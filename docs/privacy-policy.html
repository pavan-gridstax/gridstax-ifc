Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how Indian Financial Calculator (“the App”) handles information when you use the App.

Summary

  • The App does not require you to create an account.
  • Some information may be collected by third-party advertising services (Google AdMob) to show ads and measure performance.
  • The App stores some settings locally on your device using on-device storage (e.g., preferences).

Information We Collect

1) Information you provide

The App may allow you to enter values (e.g., amount, interest rate, tenure) to perform calculations. These values are used to calculate results inside the App. The App is designed to work without requiring you to submit this data to a server owned by us.

2) On-device storage (local)

The App may store some data locally on your device (for example, language settings, app preferences, and usage counters) using local storage mechanisms (such as AsyncStorage). This data stays within the app sandbox on your device.

3) Advertising / analytics data (third parties)

The App uses Google AdMob (Google Mobile Ads SDK) to display ads. AdMob may automatically collect certain information to provide, personalize (if enabled), and measure ads, and to prevent fraud/abuse.

Examples of data AdMob may process can include (depending on device settings, region, and ad configuration):

  • Advertising ID (such as AAID) and other device identifiers
  • IP address, device information, app interactions, and approximate location (derived from IP)
  • Ad engagement events (e.g., impressions, clicks)
  • Diagnostic information for ad delivery and fraud prevention

How We Use Information

  • To provide app features: calculate and display results based on the inputs you enter.
  • To store preferences: remember settings like language and other app options on your device.
  • To show ads: serve and measure advertisements via AdMob.
  • To improve stability: basic diagnostics may be used by third-party SDKs for reliability and fraud prevention.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell your personal information. However, the App uses third-party services (such as Google AdMob) that may collect and process information as described above. These services operate under their own privacy policies.

Exporting / Sharing

If the App includes features like exporting or sharing (for example, sharing a PDF or result summary), the content you choose to export/share is initiated by you and sent through the Android sharing system to the apps you select. We do not automatically send your data to our servers.

Your Choices

  • Ad personalization controls: you can reset or limit ad personalization via your device settings.
  • Uninstall: you can stop all data collection by uninstalling the App.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take appropriate steps.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect information within the scope of the App. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.
